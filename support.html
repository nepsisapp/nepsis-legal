Frequently Asked Questions

  • I purchased Nepsis, but it says I haven't paid.
    Open the app, go to your Profile, and tap "Restore Purchases." This re-checks your purchase with the App Store or Google Play and restores your access. If it still doesn't work, email us with the address you used to sign up.
  • I switched phones or reinstalled the app. How do I get my access back?
    Log in with the same account you originally signed up with, then use "Restore Purchases" from your Profile page. Your lifetime access is tied to your account, not your device.
  • How do I delete my account?
    Open the app, go to Profile, and select "Delete Account." This permanently removes your account and associated data.
  • How do I request a refund?
    Refunds are handled directly by Apple or Google. For App Store purchases, visit reportaproblem.apple.com. For Google Play purchases, visit your Google Play account and find the order under Order History.
  • Something in the app isn't working.
    Email us at hellonepsis@gmail.com with a short description of the issue, your device type, and your account email, and we'll help as soon as we can.
